Till? Jake starts showing photos of his food.
Where? Amsterdam
Costs? We will cover the venue, some snacks, and your first two drinks.

Sounds good right? If you want to come, tell us on Twitter or send Michiel an e-mail. We are also looking for a few people to give a so called lightning talk—these are short talks that last anywhere between 5 and 10 minutes; so if you're up for that, please tell us. If all goes as planned, these lightning talks will start around 14:30.

Accessibility

You can roll right into the role=drinks venue! There is an elevator to take you to the second floor—and quite possibly also back down again. Guide animals—be it dogs or dinosaurs—are also welcome. There is a park right outside where you can walk your dinosaur to go poop.

A Dutch sign language (NGT) interpreter will be present for the lightning talks. We do ask you bring your own interpreter for the socialising part of role=drinks.
